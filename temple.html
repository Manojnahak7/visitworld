Angkor Wat

Angkor Wat is a Hindu-Buddhist temple complex in Cambodia, originally built as a Hindu temple dedicated to the god Vishnu during the 12th century. It later transformed into a Buddhist temple. The temple is renowned for its grand architecture, including a moat and outer wall, three rectangular galleries, and a central quincunx of towers. It is admired for its extensive bas-reliefs and numerous devatas adorning its walls. The modern name Angkor Wat means "Temple City" in Khmer. The original name of the temple was Vrah Visnuuloka or Parama Visnuuloka, meaning "the sacred dwelling of Vishnu." read more..

Swaminarayan Akshardham (New Jersey)

The BAPS Shri Swaminarayan Mandir in Robbinsville is a Hindu place of worship built by the BAPS Swaminarayan Sanstha and consecrated by Pramukh Swami Maharaj. Constructed of hand-carved Italian Carrara marble, Indian pink stone, and limestone, it follows guidelines outlined in ancient Vedas, or Hindu scriptures. The mandir is open daily to visitors and for worship (darshan), and the complex also includes a congregation hall.The Akshardham mandir, inaugurated on October 8, 2023, is the world's 2nd largest Hindu temple, standing at 191 feet (58 m) high. It is located 62 miles (99 km) south of New York City. read more..

Prem Mandir, Vrindavan

Prem Mandir is a Hindu temple in Vrindavan, Uttar Pradesh, India, dedicated to Radha Krishna and Sita Ram. Established by Jagadguru Shri Kripalu Ji Maharaj, it is maintained by Jagadguru Kripalu Parishat. The temple complex covers 22 hectares and features depictions of four leelas of Shri Krishna. Construction started in 2001, with the inauguration in 2012 at a cost of 150 crore rupees. The main temple has life-size depictions of Radha Krishna on the first level and Sita Ram on the second level. Adjacent to the temple, a large satsang hall is being constructed to accommodate 25,000 people. The complex also includes beautiful gardens and fountains. read more..

Besakih Temple

Besakih Temple, also known as Pura Besakih, is a significant pura (temple) complex located in the village of Besakih on the slopes of Mount Agung in eastern Bali, Indonesia. It holds great importance in Balinese Hinduism and is considered the largest and holiest temple on the island. The temple complex consists of 23 separate but interconnected temples, with Pura Penataran Agung being the largest and most important among them. The temple is constructed on six levels, terraced up the slope, and features a candi bentar (split gateway) at the entrance, leading to the Kori Agung, which serves as the gateway to the second courtyard. read more..

Jagannath Temple, Puri

The Jagannath Temple in Puri, Odisha, is indeed a significant Hindu temple dedicated to Jagannath, a form of Vishnu. It is renowned for its annual Ratha Yatra, or chariot festival, during which the deities are pulled on elaborately decorated chariots. The temple's architecture and rituals hold deep spiritual and cultural significance, attracting devotees from various Hindu traditions. The temple is also associated with several prominent saints and scholars in Hinduism, further enhancing its religious importance.readmore..
